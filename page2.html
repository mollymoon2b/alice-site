The Forney Library reopend in 2017 after 2 years of repairs and extension work. On this occasion, the library asked us to design a new identity to modernize their ageing image and attract a new public. We imagined a colourfull universe based on some architectural elements of the librairy and the notion of rebirth.


The project includes a corporate identity and a website.
