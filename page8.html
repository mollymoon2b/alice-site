Motion Design

Art Installation

During the Summer of 2018, the Montfort theater invited the theater and movie company La Comète. They mostly work with a mode of filmmaking in which the film’s realization becomes subject of an experiential show called Metacinema.


We had to pay tribute to the Metacinema through a transmedia instalation around Ed Wood, a 1994 American black and white period comedy-drama film directed and produced by Tim Burton,about the cult filmmaker Ed Wood.

In collaboration with Kevin Mikhail, Elyse Niezgoda Hamdi and Camille Ten Horn.
