Editorial

In her book Les Années, published in 2008, Annie Ernaux retraces 60 years of collective history through her personal memories, revived by the consultation of family photographs. This work reflects on the very nature of these images as an epitaph and on the words that connect them.

It was an opportunity to question: How to materialize the literary extract in a form other than the book that holds it? What photographic corpus to dialogue with the original text to pay tribute ? The editorial object that result from it aims to make Annie Ernaux's words live differently.

24x15, 40 pages.
