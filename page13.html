Typeface

Editorial

Lasdun is a modular typeface and an interactive installation inspired by brutalist architecture. This project was made for the exhibition « Exposer, Explorer » at the Bibiothèque Forney in Paris.


The installation we presented at the Forney Library is designed as an experience involving the viewer in the creative process of composition and typographic construction. The modularity of Lasdun allows the user to understand the composition of typographic charactersin a fun way. The installation also  has a tutorial video showing the creative possibilities offered.
